# NepalTrust Terms of Service
**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-01

Welcome to NepalTrust. By using our platform, you agree to these terms, governed by the **E-commerce Act 2025** and **NRB Payment Systems Directive 2024**.

## 1. Escrow Service
NepalTrust acts as a neutral third-party middleman. We hold funds in a licensed Class-A bank escrow account until the "DELIVERED" or "COMPLETED" state is reached.

## 2. KYC Mandatory
No user may create or fund a vault without completing the mandatory KYC verification process. Submission of fraudulent documents will result in permanent suspension and reporting to the NRB.

## 3. The 7-Day Rule (Auto-Refund)
Per E-commerce Guidelines 2026, if a seller fails to ship an item within **7 days** of funding, the system will automatically trigger a refund to the buyer.

## 4. Dispute Resolution
- Users may raise a dispute at any time before the vault is marked "COMPLETED."
- Evidence (unboxing video, photos) is mandatory for dispute evaluation.
- NepalTrust Admin decisions on disputes are final for platform fund distribution.

## 5. Fees
NepalTrust charges a platform fee (as specified in the app during vault creation) for providing the escrow service. This fee is non-refundable once the vault is funded.

## 6. Prohibited Items
The platform may not be used for transactions involving:
- Illegal substances or firearms.
- Cryptocurrencies or unregistered digital assets.
- Any item prohibited under Nepalese Law.

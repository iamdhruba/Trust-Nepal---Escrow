# NepalTrust Privacy Policy
**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-01

This Privacy Policy describes how NepalTrust ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information in compliance with the **Individual Privacy Act 2018** and **Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB) Payment Systems Directives**.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide licensed escrow services, we are legally required to collect:
- **Identity Data (KYC):** Full name, Date of Birth, Government ID (NID/Passport/Citizenship), and a liveness selfie.
- **Contact Data:** Verified phone number (primary) and email address.
- **Financial Data:** Bank account details for payouts via connectIPS.
- **Transaction Data:** Vault details, payment status, and dispute history.
- **Technical Data:** IP address, device fingerprints, and browser/app usage logs.

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on:
- **Contractual Necessity:** To facilitate escrow transactions.
- **Legal Obligation:** KYC and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) requirements under NRB regulations.
- **Consent:** For notifications and non-essential communications.

## 3. Data Localization & Security
- **Localization:** KYC and transaction data are stored on servers located within the jurisdiction of Nepal, as required by the **Data Localization Amendment 2026**.
- **Encryption:** PII data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3.
- **Retention:** Financial records and audit logs are retained for **7 years** per NRB mandates.

## 4. Your Rights
Under the Individual Privacy Act 2018, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data.
- Correct inaccuracies.
- Request erasure (subject to regulatory retention requirements where we will anonymize rather than delete transaction skeletons).

## 5. Contact Us
For privacy concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@nepaltrust.com.np.

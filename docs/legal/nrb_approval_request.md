# NRB Approval Request Letter
**Date:** 2026-05-01
**To:** The Executive Director, Payment Systems Department, Nepal Rastra Bank, Baluwatar, Kathmandu.

**Subject:** Application for Payment Service Provider (PSP) License for Escrow-as-a-Service (NepalTrust).

**Respected Sir/Madam,**

We, NepalTrust Escrow Platform, hereby submit our application for a PSP license under the **Payment and Settlement Bylaw 2077**. Our platform is designed to facilitate secure e-commerce transactions in Nepal by acting as a neutral escrow intermediary.

**Technical Highlights:**
- **Audit-Chain Capability:** Our system maintains a SHA-256 hash-chained immutable audit log for every transaction state change.
- **Data Localization:** All PII and financial data are hosted on Nepal-domiciled infrastructure.
- **Security:** RS256 JWT authentication, Redlock distributed state management, and full encryption at rest.

We have partnered with **Nabil Bank Limited**, a Class-A commercial bank, to hold escrow funds. Enclosed are our technical architecture documents, security audit reports, and business plan.

We request your review and favorable consideration.
